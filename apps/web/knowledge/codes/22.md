## Common defences
- The vehicle did not re-park within the one-hour window — the council has confused two separate visits.
- The 'no return within one hour' rule does not apply to the bay type in question.
- The vehicle was not the same vehicle as the council allege (VRM misread, similar plate).

## Common rebuttals from council
- "ANPR/CCTV shows the same VRM returning" — counter with daylight photo of the actual plate vs the camera capture.

## Notes
Code 22 is comparatively rare and often appears when the council's
ANPR system has stitched together two separate visits. Always ask for
the exact times of both observations.
