## Common defences
- The bay's class signage was unclear or contradictory (eg. a sign indicating goods vehicles only without size restriction).
- VRM mismatch — the camera-issued PCN refers to a different vehicle.
- The vehicle does fall within the permitted class but the council mis-classified it.

## Common rebuttals from council
- "Sign clearly states class restriction" — counter with photo of sign legibility on the day.

## Notes
Code 23 is often used for CCTV-issued contraventions where the camera
misreads the VRM. Always compare the council's photograph against the
actual rear plate.
