## Common defences
- The maximum stay time was not clearly signed at the bay.
- A valid paid extension was active (eg. app top-up).
- The vehicle was within the 10-minute statutory grace period under TMA 2004 s.86.

## Common rebuttals from council
- "Vehicle observed in bay for longer than permitted" — counter with paid session receipts spanning the observation window.

## Notes
Code 30 cancellations frequently come from the 10-minute grace
period — many CEOs are unaware of it or apply it inconsistently.
