## Common defences
- A valid Blue Badge was displayed on the dashboard with the wheelchair symbol upwards.
- The badge slipped during the parked period (eg. door slam).
- The bay is a Blue Badge bay open to any holder (not a named/personalised bay).

## Common rebuttals from council
- "Badge not visible in CEO photographs" — counter with a current-validity photo of the badge AND a photo of the dashboard from the driver's seat.
- "Badge was not properly displayed" — counter with the Department for Transport's display guidance showing badge orientation.

## Notes
Code 40 cancellations are common when the appellant produces the
physical Blue Badge front (validity date visible) and a photo of the
clock setting. Letters from the issuing authority confirming the
badge holder's identity remove most council objections.
