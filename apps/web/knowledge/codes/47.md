## Common defences
- The vehicle stopped momentarily to set down a passenger (not parked, not waiting).
- The bus stop cage markings were faded or contradicted upright signs.
- The vehicle had broken down at the location.

## Common rebuttals from council
- "Vehicle was within the bus stop cage for X minutes" — counter with detail of the activity (passenger transfer, emergency).

## Notes
Bus stop infringements are often legitimately challengeable when the
vehicle was momentarily attending a passenger. Many CEOs and the
council adjudicator distinguish 'stopped' from 'parked' — make the
distinction clearly in the representation.
