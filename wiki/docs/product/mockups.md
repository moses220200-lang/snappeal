### Anatomy of the screen

| Region | Element | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Top bar | Logo (shield + "P") · "Park Appeal UK" · "Challenge your parking ticket in minutes" · `UK` location pill | Verb-led name with explicit scope marker. |
| Hero card | Navy panel, white headline "Start an Appeal", supportive copy, **red** primary CTA, document+shield illustration | First time the brand uses red as the action accent. |
| Capture row | Three options: **Scan Ticket** · **Upload Photos** · **Enter PCN** (manual) | Three entry paths, not just photo. |
| Latest case | PCN ref · council name (*Manchester City Council* — not London) · issue date · status pill *"Appeal in progress"* · 4-step progress timeline | Status tracking is first-class. |
| How it works | 3 steps: *Upload ticket* → *We draft your appeal* → *Submit and track* | "We" framing. Auto-submit promised. |
| Success tips | Green admonition: *"Appeals are most successful when submitted within 28 days of the issue date"* + **View tips** button | Tips library is a real surface. |
| Bottom nav | Home · Cases · **Camera (centred, accent)** · Profile | Four tabs. Capture is the central primary action. |

The mockup is the first authoritative visual reference for the app. The [audit on the next page](v0-1-mockup-audit.md) lists every place the rest of the wiki disagrees with what this screen shows.
